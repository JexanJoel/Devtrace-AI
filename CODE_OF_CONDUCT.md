## DevTrace AI Community Standards
**Building a healthy, inclusive, and elite debugging culture.**

---

</div>

## 📌 TL;DR (Executive Summary)

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of background or identity. 
- **Be Kind**: Empathy and respect are mandatory.
- **Be Professional**: No harassment, trolling, or exclusionary behavior.
- **Collaborate Fairly**: This is a space for learning and technical growth.

---

## 🛡️ Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## ✅ Standards of Excellence

> [!TIP]
> Positive behaviors that we encourage in the DevTrace AI community:

- **Empathy Over Ego**: Understand that everyone is at a different stage of their debugging journey.
- **Constructive Feedback**: Focus on the code, not the person.
- **Inclusivity**: Use welcoming language and be mindful of global perspectives.
- **Grace**: Accept responsibility for mistakes, apologize, and learn.

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

> [!WARNING]
> The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and may result in a permanent ban:

- **Harassment**: Any form of bullying, trolled comments, or personal attacks.
- **Exclusionary Language**: Use of sexualized imagery or language.
- **Doxing**: Publishing others' private information without consent.
- **Disruption**: Deliberate interference with community discussions or development.

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Correction**: Use of inappropriate language or behavior. Result: Private warning.
2. **Warning**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions. Result: Warning with consequences.
3. **Temporary Ban**: A serious violation of community standards. Result: Temporary suspension from all spaces.
4. **Permanent Ban**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation. Result: Permanent exclusion.

---

## 📢 Reporting an Incident

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it immediately:
- **Primary Contact**: Open a [Private Security Advisory](https://github.com/JexanJoel/DevTrace-AI/security/advisories/new) on GitHub.
- **Secondary Contact**: Send a direct message to [JexanJoel](https://github.com/JexanJoel) via GitHub or linked social channels.

*All reports will be kept confidential. We are committed to investigating every claim fairly and promptly.*
